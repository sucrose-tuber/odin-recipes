Weird Breakfast

Home

DESCRIPTION

My uncultured friends always describe my breakfast as "weird" when I serve this to them. In reality, it's a very common dish in Latin America. I will assume in Africa as well because the only photo I could find available for public use was written in Igbo.

INGREDIENTS

  • Banana or plaintain
  • 1 or 2 eggs, depending how hungry you are
  • Half a can of beans because I never remember to soak uncooked beans overnight
  • Oil, preferably vegetable (lighter flavor compared to others)
  • Shredded cheese
  • Tabasco sauce (red if you like spicy, green if you like flavor, or both)
